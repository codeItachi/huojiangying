Award | Hei Yiyang won the "2016 UNKNOWN/ASIA"Art Fair Grand Prix Nomination

中文
During 30th September ，2016 to 2nd October，the 2nd art fair “Unknown Asia Art Exchange Osaka 2016” was held in Osaka Japen. The fair offers various outstanding artists from all over Asia. Hei Yiyang was invited to the exhibition. The series of “I Prefer This Colorful World” artworks were awarded a Grand Prix Nomination, a Judges Prize and two Reviewer Prize.
The Judges Prize was awarded by 松尾良一(Japan), two Reviewer Prize were awarded by 金崎亮太(Japan) and 後藤哲也 (Japan).
The Founder And Host Artist of XIANGDEMEI
Lives and works in Shenzhen and Shanghai
Hei Yiyang(born in 1975 in Henan Province ) is one of the most active artistic and cultural exchange pioneers in China. He devotes himself to putting different mediums like design, contemporary art, advertising, architectural space, city, society together, to make the works interesting, meaningful and valuable.
Hei Yiyang prefers investigating the phenomena of sociology instead of impersonating it. Carring on their legacy, re-contextualizing art practice into cultural and political atmospheres constitutes the early focus of Hei’ artistic explorously. For a long period of time the artist recorded sociology with a restrained attitude, aiming to substitute Multi-dimensional thinking and subvert traditional art concepts for rational presentations. Hei Yiyang tends to interpretate an artistic kind of science, a poetic logic.
Behind each project is an extensive process of research, involving a mapping of the process through a series of collage and photography. His collage, installation, video and documentary touch upon the recurring topics of social phenomenon, conflict, humanity, time and national frontiers. Key to Hei’s interest in conflict is its interplay with time. Eventually presents the contemporary social and cultural analysis from an artist’s view.

Unknown Asia Art Exchange Osaka
(hereinafter referred to as "UNKNOWN/ASIA) was first held in 2015. Art fair in Osaka for up-and -coming artists from unrestricted categories! International creators and reviewers gather to exchange ideas and get a chance to take off to unknown Asia. The fair offers 180 teams of creators from Japan, China, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Korea, Thailand, Taiwan, Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia.
In 2015, Unknown Asia was collected huge praise, and the event on this year will be organized wider scale. Venue is at Herbis Hall in Osaka Umeda, exciting art fair will be organized at basement offive star class hotel Ritz Carton Osaka. Increase volume of participants from last year, 180 booths will be available, inviting participant from Japan, and Asian countries. It is a public fair of any genre of Art, Design, Photography, Illustration, Fashion, Installations, and more.
